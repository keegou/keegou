About

I'm not a guru.

I write things down. That's all. I don't have a system, a philosophy, or a set of rules. I just have pages. If something I've lived resonates with you — good. If it doesn't — that's also good. You have your own road to walk.

I work with an AI.

Some of what you read here was shaped in conversation with it. I don't hide that. If the thought of that bothers you, this might not be the place for you. That's okay.

I don't know you yet — but I already trust you.

I write for someone I haven't met, and I assume you're here in good faith. If that assumption ever proves wrong, I'll adjust. But I'd rather trust too early than guard too soon.

This page will change.

As I figure out what this place is, this page will change. The rest of the site will too. That's part of the process. If you're the kind of person who wants to be here while it's still being built — I'm glad you're here.

A practice, not a promise.

I write things down because it helps me see them clearly. Sometimes, after I've written something, I notice it might help someone else try it too. That's what the paid pages are — not better, just longer. Things I tried, written down so you can try them too, if you want.

I don't give advice.

I don't know your situation. I don't know what's right for you. I only know what I've lived. If you take something from this site and it helps — I'm glad. If you take something and it doesn't — I hope you leave it behind without guilt.

If this isn't for you — that's okay.

Not everything has to be for everyone. If you read something here and felt nothing, that's fine. If you came looking for answers and didn't find them, I hope you find them somewhere else. No hard feelings.

If you'd like to say hello — hello@keegou.com
