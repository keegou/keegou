On Waiting

Don't rush to prove yourself. The people who matter are not counting how fast you arrive; they're counting how much you grow along the way.

Don't let urgency rob you of presence. The thing you're chasing will still be there tomorrow — but the person you're becoming today is built in the moments you don't skip.

Don't mistake motion for progress. Sometimes the most important thing you can do is stay still and let the dust settle.

🌷

On Silence

Not every pause needs to be filled. Silence is not an absence of words; it's a presence of something deeper. Let it sit. Let it breathe. Let it tell you what words cannot.

When you stop filling every moment with noise, you create space for something real to enter. And sometimes, the realest things come not in a shout — but in a whisper that you finally have the ears to hear.

🌷

On Not Knowing

Not knowing is not a weakness. It's the only honest place to start. The people who pretend to have all the answers are often the ones most afraid of the questions.

Sit with the uncertainty. Let it teach you patience. Let it teach you that you don't need to know everything to move forward. You just need to know enough to take the next step.
